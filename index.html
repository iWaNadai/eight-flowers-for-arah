I love you
with all my heart.

Ikaw ang pahinga.

My day becomes sweeter
when I think of you.

I will long for you
like the how rain
longs for the ground.

You are the sweet sugar that
my bitter coffee needs.

With you, every moment is a sweet memory in the making.

Loving you is like breathing;
effortless and essential.

In your smile, I see something more beautiful than stars.
